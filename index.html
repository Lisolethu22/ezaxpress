About Eza Xpress

Black-owned transport company founded in 2021, providing excellent services across the Western Cape.

Our Story

Eza Xpress (PTY) LTD is a Black-owned company founded on 15 June 2021 by sole Director Xola Eza Mtwa. With expertise in all spheres of transport, Xola has steered Eza Xpress to successfully provide excellent services to various clients in the Western Cape area.

Our services include small and large scale transport needs, ranging from local travelling to long distance travelling. Our efficient, well-trained team of professional drivers and internal quality assurance processes ensure all projects meet customer requirements to perfection.

Mission

To be a fully operational business distinguished from competitors and provide private rapid transport that is effective and reliable while promoting skills development and arrive alive campaigns.

Vision

To become the market leader in transportation and travel services through innovation, quality and excellence, changing how public transport operates in our region.
